About Memeja
We believe in connecting people together through shared experiences.

Memeja helps you share your life stories with the people you care about. Through memes, you connect with, laugh with, and uplift the lives of everyone you love as you share those memorable moments that create a lasting impression. Sure, it starts with basic images and text, but we go beyond that. Memeja is all about the human connection. This is social networking with emotions.

Create and tag your friends in stories

We forge our deepest connections by sharing the human experiences with other people. Yet emotionally contextualizing stories is time-consuming and hard. Memeja makes it easy to share experiences thorugh pictorial storytelling. These are memes: bring your story to life like never before; tag your friends and connect through the human experience with greater intimacy.
